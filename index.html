What Is Mixology?

Mixology is a term used to describe the art of mixing cocktail drink recipes. A mixologist is a person who mixes and invents cocktail drinks. All mixologist are bartenders, but not all bartenders are mixologists.

We are not just regular Bartenders, we are the best of the best at our craft. We are the masters of the art of Mixology.
